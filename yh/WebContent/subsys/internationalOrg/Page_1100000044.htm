International Federation of Societies of Cosmetic Chemists (IFSCC)
Fdration internationale des socits des chimistes cosmticiens
Internationale Fderation der Gesellschaften der Kosmetik-Chemiker

URL: http://www.ifscc.org/

History
8 Sep 1959, Brussels (Belgium). Amended Constitution approved: 1 Sep 1961, Copenhagen (Denmark); 26 Sep 1970, Barcelona (Spain).
Aims
Advance cosmetic science; encourage research and coordinate the work of national societies; help research workers obtain information; develop standardized procedures for analysis of raw materials and finished products and for estimating efficiency of products.
Structure
Annual Council, composed of delegates from member societies (1 delegate per 100 scientific members) elects Praesidium.
Languages
English.
Staff
2 paid.
Finances
Members' dues: Swiss Fr 7 per member of each society.
Relations with non-governmental organizations
Links indicated by: Asian Society of Cosmetic Scientists (ASCS).
Activities
Holds international congresses; sponsors awards for achievement in research.
