International Softbill Society (ISS)

    History
    1986.
    Aims
    Improve the propagation of softbilled birds, i e birds characterized by dietary preference for fruits, insects, and nectar, and include toucans, turacos, tanagers, jays, barbets, hummingbirds and similar species.
    Finances
    Members' dues.
    Activities
    Disseminates information on the housing, diet, health and natural history of softbilled birds. Organizes annual meetings.
    Publications
    The Honeycreeper (periodical). Membership Directory/Registry (annual). Newsletters.
    Members
    Life; Institutional; Supporting (membership mainly in USA), in 8 countries:

     • Americas: Canada, Colombia, Mexico, USA.

     • Australia: Australia.

     • Europe: Netherlands, Norway, UK.
